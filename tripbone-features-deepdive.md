## 1. AI Tour Generation: One-Click Package Creation
**Create comprehensive, customized itineraries in seconds instead of hours.**

### 💡 Core Value Proposition
*   **Legacy Pain:** Building custom itineraries manually takes 45 to 90 minutes of researching, editing, copy-pasting images, formatting PDFs, and pricing components.
*   **Tripbone Solution:** Simply insert your destination, duration, style prompt, and guest preferences. Our proprietary AI engine instantly drafts a day-by-day itinerary down to the hour, ready to book.

### 🔄 How It Works (Step-by-Step)
1.  **Input Parameters:** The admin fills out a quick prompt (e.g., *"3-day romantic honeymoon in East Bali with snorkeling and private jeeps"*).
2.  **Context-Aware Generation:** Tripbone’s AI accesses your pre-configured inventory, pricing sheets, and geographic logs to assemble the perfect trip.
3.  **Instant Production:** It produces beautiful markdown details, highlight cards, inclusion summaries (what's included/excluded), and passenger details instantly.
4.  **Publish to Web:** 1-click publishes the generated tour directly to your live travel booking website, fully formatted with booking buy-buttons.

*   *Telemetry Tag:* `[ AUTO-PACKAGING MODULE v2.5 ]`

### 📊 Pain Points Solved & Revenue Metrics
*   **7x Speed Increase:** Reduces itinerary creation workflows from over an hour to less than 10 seconds.
*   **Zero Typos:** Eliminates manual copying errors, duplicate reservations, and outdated pricing on custom documents.

## 3. WhatsApp Automation: Combined Crew & Guest Telemetry
**Keep your travelers, reservation desks, and tour guides instantly aligned without typing a single word.**

### 💡 Core Value Proposition
*   **Legacy Pain:** Keeping drivers posted and guests updated requires constant copying, pasting, and manually typing messages on mobile phones.
*   **Tripbone Solution:** Selecting a driver in your console triggers an instant, simultaneous background dispatch loop that briefs both your team and your customer on the channel they check most.

### 🔄 How It Works (Step-by-Step)
1.  **The Assign Trigger:** The operations admin selects a driver from the Booking Manager dropdown.
2.  **Instant Guide Briefing (WhatsApp):** Behind the scenes, a copy-formatted briefing (Guest Name, Hotel Room, Pickup Time, Passenger Count, and Collect Amount) fires straight to the guide's WhatsApp.
3.  **Instant Traveler Alert (WhatsApp):** At the exact same millisecond, a WhatsApp confirmation triggers to the guest: *"Hi Sarah! Meet your guide Putu for your Nusa Penida Tour tomorrow. Contact: +6281..."*
4.  **Admin Broadcast:** All actions are instantly saved into the audit logs with high-contrast indicator dots.
