# Tripbone Affiliate Program
**"Grow Together. Scale Exponentially."**

Tripbone is built for a collaborative travel economy. Our affiliate module allows you to turn your partners, influencers, and happy guests into a global sales force.

---

## 🤝 1. For Tourism Partners
- **Agent Portal:** Travel agencies and hotel concierges can have their own login to book tours for their guests.
- **Dynamic Commision Tracking:** Automatically track which agent made which booking and calculate their cut instantly.
- **White-Label Agent Vouchers:** Agents can print confirmation vouchers with *your* branding but *their* reference ID.

---

## 📱 2. For Influencers & Creators
- **Unique Referral Links:** Give influencers a 5-10% discount code to share with their followers.
- **Transparent Analytics:** Influencers can log in (optional) to see how many bookings they've generated in real-time.
- **AI Content Support:** Provide influencers with AI-generated media kits and taglines to make their promotion effortless.

---

## 🎁 3. The "Guest Referral" Engine
- **Review for Reward:** Incentivize guests to leave a 5-star review in exchange for a discount on their *next* adventure or for a friend.
- **Viral Sharing:** Every AI Travel Plan shared via social media includes your affiliate tracking to ensure you get credited for the traffic.

---

## 📊 4. Admin Management
- **One-Click Payouts:** Review all pending commissions and mark them as paid from one dashboard.
- **Custom Percentages:** Set different commission rates for different partners (e.g., 20% for top-tier agencies, 10% for new influencers).
- **Fraud Prevention:** Built-in safeguards to ensure referrals are legitimate and bookings are confirmed before payouts are authorized.
