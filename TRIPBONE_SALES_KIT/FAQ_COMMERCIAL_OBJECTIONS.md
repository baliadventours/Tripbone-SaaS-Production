# Tripbone FAQ & Objection Handling
**"Closing the Gap Between Interest and Purchase"**

Use these pre-written answers for customer inquiries on Tripbone.com or in your sales emails.

---

## ❓ Q: Do I need to be a 'tech person' to use Tripbone?
**A:** No. If you can use a Facebook page or a WhatsApp group, you can manage Tripbone. The AI handles the complex writing and technical SEO, while the dashboard is designed for rapid, intuitive use.

---

## ❓ Q: Where is my data hosted?
**A:** Your site is hosted on global edge networks (Firebase/Google Cloud). This ensures that whether your customer is in London or Sydney, your Bali tours load in under one second.

---

## ❓ Q: Is it really 'Zero Commission'?
**A:** Yes. Unlike other platforms that take $2-$5 per person, we don't touch your revenue. You pay for the license, and 100% of the customer's payment goes directly to your bank account via your chosen gateway.

---

## ❓ Q: Can I use my own domain?
**A:** Absolutely. We provide free SSL and simple instructions to point your domain (e.g., `adventurebali.com`) to your Tripbone engine.

---

## ❓ Q: Does it work on Mobile?
**A:** Tripbone is "Mobile-First." 85% of modern travel bookings happen on phones. We’ve optimized every button, form, and map to ensure a flawless experience for guests on the move.

---

## ❓ Q: What if I have 500 existing tours on another site?
**A:** We offer a bulk CSV import tool and a dedicated migration service. Our AI can also scan your old site and "re-write" your tours for Tripbone to ensure you keep your SEO ranking.

---

## ❓ Q: Can I have multiple currencies?
**A:** Yes. Sell in USD, AUD, EUR, or IDR. The system can automatically handle conversion or allow you to set fixed rates for different countries.
