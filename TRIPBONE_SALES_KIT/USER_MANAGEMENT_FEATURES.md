# Tripbone – Multi-Role User Management
**"An Ecosystem for Owners, Staff, and Partners"**

Tripbone is built for growth. From a solo operator to a multi-national agency, our user system scales with your permissions needs.

---

## 👥 1. Multi-Role Ecosystem
- **The Super Admin:** Full control over settings, finances, and system configuration.
- **The Operations Manager:** Focused on bookings, dispatch, and guide assignments without seeing sensitive financial data.
- **The Staff/Agent Account:** Restricted access to check booking statuses and handle customer inquiries.

---

## 🤝 2. The Supplier/Vendor Portal
- **Independent Inventory Management:** Allow local partners (e.g., a boat company or a cooking school) to log in and manage their own specific tours.
- **Limited Financial View:** Suppliers only see their own bookings and their share of the payout.
- **Admin Approval Gate:** Every change a supplier makes to their tour data can be flagged for Admin review before it goes live.

---

## 👤 3. Customer Retention & Profiles
- **Guest Dashboard:** Travelers can log in to view their booking history, status, and saved AI Travel Plans.
- **Wishlist & Favorites:** Registered users can curate their "Dream Bali Trip," giving you valuable data on high-intent leads.
- **Verified Review System:** Only guests with a "Completed" booking can leave reviews, ensuring 100% authenticity and trust.

---

## 🛡️ 4. Security & Authentication
- **Social Login Integration:** Seamless login via Google or WhatsApp.
- **Secure PII Isolation:** Personally Identifiable Information (emails, phone numbers) is protected by hardened Firestore rules.
- **Activity Logs:** Track who changed what in the system for ultimate accountability.
