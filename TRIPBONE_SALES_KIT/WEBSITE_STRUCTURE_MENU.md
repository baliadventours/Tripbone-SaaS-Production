# Tripbone.com – Site Structure & Menu Architecture
**"Engineering the Path from Visitor to Owner"**

To maximize conversion, your website needs to be a balance of "High-Tech" (AI) and "High-Utility" (Operations). Below is the recommended sitemap and homepage flow.

---

## 🧭 1. Navigation Menu (Header)
*Clean, minimalist, and sticky.*

- **Features (Dropdown):**
    - **AItinerary Planner** (The Showstopper)
    - **Surgical Inventory** (The Backbone)
    - **Operational Logistics** (The Engine)
    - **AI Content Machine** (The Marketer)
    - **Design Builder** (The Architect)
- **Success Stories:** Case studies and operator testimonials.
- **Pricing:** Transparent, one-time payment options.
- **Resources (Dropdown):**
    - Blog (Travel Insights)
    - Support & Documentation
    - Affiliate Program (Scalability)
- **Login** (Text Link)
- **[Buy Now]** (Primary Action Button - High Contrast)

## 🏠 2. Homepage Architecture (The Scroll Path)

### Section 1: The AI Hero
- **Visual:** A high-quality mock-up of the mobile app showing a plan being generated by **AItinerary**.
- **Headline:** "The Backbone of AI-Powered Tour Booking."
- **Sub-headline:** "Launch, host, and manage your modern AI travel brand. Cut commissions, automate operations, and scale with the intelligence of Google Gemini."
- **Primary CTA:** [Get Tripbone Today]
- **Secondary CTA:** [Watch Demo]

### Section 2: Trust Signals (The Strip)
- "Trusted by 500+ activity providers worldwide."
- Scrolling marquee of logos (or simply destination names like Bali, Kyoto, Alpine Peaks).

### Section 3: The "AItinerary" Experience (Interactive)
- A dedicated spot showing how the AI agent interviews guests.
- **Copy:** "Ditch the search fatigue. AItinerary builds custom, high-converting plans in seconds, linking directly to your inventory."

### Section 4: The Enterprise Pillars (The 4-Card Grid)
- **Inventory:** Live occupancy & tiered pricing.
- **Operations:** WhatsApp dispatch & OTA extraction.
- **Marketing:** AI blog engine & SEO automation.
- **Design:** Modular Builder (Endless UI possibilities).
- **Continuity:** 1-Click Backup & Instant Restore (Your business's safety net).
- **Finance:** Automated vendor payouts & commission tracking.

### Section 5: The "Comparison" Table
- Tripbone vs. Marketplaces (Viator/Klook) vs. Old SaaS.
- **Highlight:** 0% Booking Fees vs. 25%.

### Section 6: Real-World ROI (Case Study)
- **Feature:** Bali Adventours.
- **Headline:** "How one agency scaled from 5 to 50 tours while cutting admin time by 40%."
- **Visual:** Graph showing revenue growth.

### Section 7: Pricing Grid (Transparent Value)
- **System Only ($99):** The Core Engine.
- **System + Installation ($299):** White-Glove Setup (Most Popular).
- **Enterprise Build ($1999):** Full customization.

### Section 8: Final CTA (The FOMO)
- **Headline:** "Transform your agency with Tripbone."
- **Input:** [Enter Email Address] -> [Get Started Now]
