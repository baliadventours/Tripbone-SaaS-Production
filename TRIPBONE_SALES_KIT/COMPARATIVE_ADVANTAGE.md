# Tripbone vs. The World: Comparative Advantage
**"Why Savvy Operators are Leaving Marketplaces Behind"**

Selling Tripbone requires positioning it against "Old World" booking systems and "High Commission" marketplaces. Use these talking points.

---

## 1. Tripbone vs. Marketplaces (Viator, Klook, GetYourGuide)
- **The Commission Trap:** Marketplaces take 20-30%. Tripbone takes 0%. 
- **Data Ownership:** On marketplaces, you don't own the guest's email. On Tripbone, every guest is a lifelong lead for your remarketing.
- **Brand Identity:** On marketplaces, you are "Company ABC." On Tripbone, you are a premium, independent brand.

---

## 2. Tripbone vs. Traditional SaaS (Rezdy, FareHarbor, Peek)
- **The Fee Structure:** Most SaaS charge $100-$500/month plus booking fees. Tripbone is a "Pay Once, Own Forever" model.
- **AI Native vs. AI Added:** Traditional systems are trying to "add" AI. Tripbone was *built* around the **AItinerary** Planner, making it significantly smarter and more conversational.
- **Speed & Weight:** Traditional systems are often 10+ years old and slow. Tripbone is built on the latest React/Vite stack for sub-second load times.

---

## 3. Tripbone vs. Generic Site Builders (Shopify, Wix, WordPress)
- **Travel-Specific Logic:** Shopify doesn't understand "Pick-up Zones" or "Adult/Child Pricing Tiers" out of the box. You need 50 plugins to make it work. Tripbone has this in its DNA.
- **The Logistics Gap:** Generic builders don't have a "Guide Dispatch" or "OTA Extraction" tool. Tripbone is a business operation tool, not just a website.
- **Maintenance:** WordPress requires constant updates and plugin fixes. Tripbone is a clean, serverless architecture with almost zero maintenance.
