# Tripbone – Finance & Revenue Module
**"Track Every Cent. Automate Every Payout."**

Tripbone isn't just a booking engine; it’s a financial ledger designed to handle the multi-layered economics of the travel industry.

---

## 📊 1. Revenue & Performance Insights
- **The Profit Dashboard:** Real-time visualization of Gross Revenue, Net Profit, and Commission totals.
- **Occupancy Yield:** Analyze which tours are underperforming and which are your "Cash Cows" with heat-map occupancy data.
- **Comparison Reports:** Compare this month's performance against last year to track growth.

---

## 💸 2. Commission & Payout Management
- **Automated Vendor Calculations:** System automatically splits bookings into "Agency Commission" and "Vendor Payout" based on your pre-set rules.
- **Payout Payer:** Track which suppliers have been paid and which payouts are pending.
- **Tax & Fee Configuration:** Add VAT, Service Charges, or Booking Fees as separate line items for transparent accounting.

---

## 🎟️ 3. Growth & Discount Engine
- **Smart Promo Codes:** Create percentage-based or fixed-amount discounts with specific rules:
    - Minimum spend requirements.
    - Expiry dates.
    - "First-booking" only rules.
- **Last-Minute Deals:** Instantly mark specific inventory dates as "On Sale" to fill empty seats 24 hours before departure.

---

## 🧾 4. Automated Financial Documents
- **Pro-Grade Invoicing:** Professional PDF invoices generated automatically the second a payment is logged.
- **Receipt Automation:** Automated email receipts sent to guests with your brand's unique logo and information.
- **Refund Logging:** Track cancellations and partial refunds within the system for accurate end-of-year reporting.
