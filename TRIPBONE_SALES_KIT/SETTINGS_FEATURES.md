# Tripbone – Global Settings & Configuration
**"Your Brand. Your Rules."**

Tripbone is designed to be invisible. We provide the engine; you provide the brand. Personalize every aspect of the experience from one central panel.

---

## 🎨 1. White-Label Brand Control
- **Custom Identity:** Upload your logos (Primary, Icon, Dark Mode) and set your brand's core colors with one-click theme updates.
- **Font Strategy:** Choose from premium typography pairings (Modern Sans, Technical Mono, Elegant Serif) to match your market positioning.
- **Domain Mapping:** Host your storefront on your own domain (e.g., `tours.yourbrand.com`) for a professional customer journey.

---

## ⚙️ 2. Core System Preferences
- **Currency & Localization:** Set your primary currency and date formats to match your local market or your primary customer base.
- **Global Contact Logic:** Set centralized WhatsApp numbers, Support Emails, and Office Locations that update across the entire site instantly.
- **Social Integration:** Link your Instagram, TikTok, and TripAdvisor profiles once and have them perfectly formatted in every footer and blog post.
- **1-Click Backup & Restore:** Access your business's "Undo" button—capture instant snapshots and restore your entire site from one central button.

---

## 🛠️ 3. Developer & Integration Tools
- **Webhook Ready:** Connect Tripbone to external tools (Zapier, Email Marketing) for advanced automation workflows.
- **Analytics Integration:** Drop in your Google Analytics or Meta Pixel codes to track conversion rates with surgical precision.
- **Custom Scripts:** Add your own live chat widgets or tracking scripts without touching the core code.

---

## 🌍 4. Advanced SEO & Sitemap Control
- **Dynamic Sitemap:** Every time you add a tour, Tripbone regenerates your sitemap.xml to ensure Google crawls new pages instantly.
- **Robots.txt management:** Control how search engines index your site.
- **Redirect Engine:** Manage 301 redirects to preserve link juice when you update tour names or slugs.
