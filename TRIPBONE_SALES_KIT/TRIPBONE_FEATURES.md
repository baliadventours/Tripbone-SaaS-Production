# Tripbone: The Full Feature Ecosystem
**The Backbone of AI-Powered Tour Booking**

Tripbone is a complete, enterprise-grade operating system for modern travel businesses. Below is the exhaustive breakdown of the technical and operational capabilities of the platform.

---

## 📦 1. Inventory Management
*The foundation of your supply chain.*

### A. Dynamic Catalog Engine
- **Hierarchical Categorization:** Organize by Destination (e.g., Bali), Zone (e.g., Ubud), and Activity Type (e.g., Adventure).
- **Tour Cloning:** Duplicate complex tour setups in one click.
- **Rich Media Support:** Integrated high-density gallery management with optimized image rendering.

### B. Live Occupancy Control
- **Real-Time Sync:** Centralized database ensuring zero overbooking.
- **Multi-Slot Logic:** Manage daily, morning, afternoon, and night sessions per tour.
- **Bulk Setup Tool:** Manage capacity and blackouts across date ranges for hundreds of tours simultaneously.

### C. Advanced Pricing Logic
- **Quantity-Based Tiers:** Custom pricing for Solo, 2-5 pax, or large groups.
- **Age-Based Tiers:** Automated Adult/Child/Infant price calculation.
- **Add-on Revenue Engine:** Sell GoPro rentals, premium insurance, or private transport upgrades during checkout.

---

## 📅 2. Booking Management
*Turning inquiries into confirmed expeditions.*

### A. Operational Dashboard
- **Unified Booking View:** Filter, search, and manage every booking from a single high-speed interface.
- **Manual Booking Creation:** Rapidly log WhatsApp or walk-in customers into the system.
- **OTA Extraction:** Special "Paste & Parse" feature to instantly import data from Viator, Klook, and GetYourGuide.

### B. Post-Booking Automation
- **WhatsApp Integrations:** Automated confirmation messages with meeting point links.
- **Digital Invoicing:** Pro-grade PDF invoices generated instantly.
- **Tracking System:** A dedicated client-side "Booking Tracker" for customers to check status without calling you.

### C. Resource Allocation
- **Guide/Driver Assignment:** Link staff to specific departures.
- **Vendor Portal:** Curated access for suppliers to manage their own specific tours.

---

## ✍️ 3. Content Generation
*Eliminating writer's block with Google Gemini AI.*

### A. AI Tour Architect
- **Prompt-to-Tour:** Input basic highlights; the AI generates professional descriptions, itineraries, and "What to Bring" lists.
- **Sentiment Analysis:** AI scans reviews to highlight key selling points automatically.

### B. Automated Blogging
- **Topic Engine:** Generates trending travel topics based on your inventory.
- **SEO-First Articles:** Full blog posts generated with internal links to your tours.

### C. Visual Strategy
- **AI Banner Generation:** Integrated image synthesis for hero sections and blog thumbnails.

## 🌐 5. Advanced Design Builder
*The "Endless Possibilities" engine.*

### A. Modular Layouts
- **Bento Grid Architecture:** Drag-and-drop structural elements (Tours, Reviews, AI Prompts) to build custom pages.
- **Responsive by Design:** Every custom layout automatically optimizes for Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop.

### B. Brand Identity Control
- **Global Stylist:** 1-Click color and typography swaps across the entire platform.
- **Visual Staging:** Preview design changes in real-time before going live.

---

## 🗺️ 6. AI Planner (The "AItinerary" Engine)
*The platform's showstopper feature for travelers.*

### A. Conversational Discovery
- **Interview-Style Planning:** Users chat with "AItinerary" about their vibe, budget, and companions.
- **Day-by-Day Logic:** AI builds a complete, chronologically sound travel plan for entire trips (e.g., "7 Days in Bali").

### B. Inventory Matching
- **Smart Recommendations:** The AI planner prioritize *your* tours when building the user's itinerary.
- **Direct Checkout:** Every AI-suggested activity links directly to your booking engine.

### C. Lead Capture
- **Plan Sharing:** Users must provide Email/WhatsApp to "save" their master plan, creating high-intent leads.

## 🤖 7. AI Chatbot (Client-Side Agent)
*24/7 Virtual Concierge.*

### A. Real-Time Inquiries
- **Catalog Navigation:** Answers "What adventures do you have in Ubud?" with direct links.
- **Booking Support:** Helps users find their booking status or price tiers via chat.

### B. Operational Offloading
- **FAQ Handling:** Answers common questions about gear, pick-up times, and weather.
