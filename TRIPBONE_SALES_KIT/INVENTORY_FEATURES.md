# Surgical Inventory – The Backbone
**"Real-Time Precision for Fluid Operations"**

Inventory is more than just a list of tours; it is the lifeblood of your business. Tripbone’s inventory module is engineered to handle the complexities of real-world travel, from high-frequency daily departures to exclusive, vendor-supplied expeditions.

---

## 🏗️ Core Architecture & High-Density Control
Tripbone provides a "Surgical Control Center" that allows administrators to monitor every moving part of their supply chain in real-time.
- **Enterprise Tour Catalog:** A high-performance grid and list view designed for hundreds of SKUs. Categorize, tag, and organize tours with ease.
- **Clone Engine:** Speed up your expansion. Duplicate existing tours with one click to create variations or similar routes without repetitive data entry.
- **Multilingual & SEO Readiness:** Every inventory item is ready for global distribution with fields for meta-descriptions, custom slugs, and optimized content blocks.

---

## ⚡ Live Occupancy & Availability Engine
Never risk your reputation with an overbooked tour. Our real-time engine handles the math for you.
- **Instant Occupancy Tracking:** Visual indicators show real-time seat counts for every departure date. 
- **Time-Slot Precision:** Manage "Daily" departures or complex multi-slot schedules (e.g., Sunrise, Midday, Sunset) within a single tour configuration.
- **Automatic Buffer Management:** Set expiration windows to close bookings automatically (e.g., 2 hours before departure) to give your operations team breathing room.

---

## 🛠️ Bulk Availability Ops (The Scalability Secret)
Managing peak season or holidays shouldn't take all day. Our Bulk Setup tool allows for macro-level adjustments in seconds.
- **Date-Range Blockers:** Instantly close all tours for Nyepi, Christmas, or maintenance periods across months.
- **Seasonal Capacity Shifting:** Increase capacity for June-August and reduce it for the rainy season with a few clicks.
- **Safety Overwrites:** Update capacity while preserving existing booking counts—the system intelligently ensures you never "adjust" yourself into an overbooked state.

## 💰 Flexible Multi-Tier Pricing
Tripbone supports complex financial models that mirror how tour operators actually sell.
- **Quantity-Based Scaling:** Set different prices for Solo travelers vs. Couples or Large Groups.
- **Dynamic Age Tiers:** Distinct pricing logic for Adults, Children, and Infants.
- **Currency Agnostic:** Display and sell in multiple currencies with automated conversion rates.
- **Dynamic Add-Ons (Revenue Booster):** Maximize Average Order Value by allowing customers to add GoPros, transfers, or premium lunches during the booking flow. Each add-on is integrated directly into the inventory availability.
