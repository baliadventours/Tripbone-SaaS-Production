# Operational Logistics – The Engine
**"Automating the Chaos of Daily Dispatch"**

Tripbone's operations module is built for the "boots-on-the-ground" reality of tour businesses. It bridges the gap between a booking confirmation and a happy guest on the tour.

---

## 📋 1. Revolutionary Booking Management
- **The Unified Control Center:** A high-speed, filterable list of every booking. instantly see who is confirmed, who is pending, and who has been picked up.
- **OTA Data Extraction (The Game Changer):** Stop manual entry. Copy a booking confirmation email from Viator, GetYourGuide, or Klook, paste it into Tripbone, and watch as our AI extracts the guest name, date, pax, and tour details instantly.
- **Manual Booking Overwrites:** Easily log walk-in guests or WhatsApp inquiries with a streamlined "Quick-Book" interface.

---

## 🚚 2. Dispatch & Staff Management
- **Automated Guide Assignment:** Link specific guides or drivers to tour departures. Guides can receive their schedules automatically.
- **Instant Dispatch Notifications:** One-click WhatsApp dispatch. Send the guest list, pick-up locations, and special requests to your drivers in a formatted mobile-friendly message.
- **Manifest Generation:** Export professional guest manifests (PDF/Print) for airport pick-ups or boat departures.

---

## 📡 3. Real-Time Tracking & Notifications
- **Booking Tracker (Customer Side):** Every booking gets a unique ID. Guests can visit your "Track My Booking" page to see their status, pick-up time, and guide details without ever emailing your support team.
- **Status Workflows:** Move bookings through logical stages: `Pending` → `Confirmed` → `Picking Up` → `In Progress` → `Completed`.
- **System-Wide Alerts:** Desktop and mobile notifications for new bookings, cancellations, or last-minute changes.

---

## 🗺️ 4. Logistics & Pickup Logic
- **Interactive Pickup Mapping:** Integrated with OpenStreetMap to show exactly where guests are located.
- **Zone-Based Logistics:** Group pickups by area (e.g., "Kuta/Seminyak Zone") to optimize driver routes and reduce fuel costs.
- **Special Request Handling:** Dedicated fields for dietary requirements, hotel room numbers, and flight details that follow the booking all the way to the guide's phone.
