# Tripbone – 1-Click Backup & Business Continuity
**"Your Data is Your Greatest Asset. Protect it Effortlessly."**

The travel industry is fast-moving. Between last-minute tour changes and high-volume seasonal bookings, your data is constantly evolving. Tripbone ensures you never lose a single byte of your business history with our enterprise-grade backup suite.

---

## 🛡️ 1. The 1-Click Safety Net
Mistakes happen. A staff member might accidentally delete a tour or overwrite a critical setting. With Tripbone, you have an "Undo" button for your entire business.
- **Snapshot Technology:** Instantly capture the state of your entire database—bookings, tours, settings, and blog posts—with a single click from the dashboard.
- **Visual History:** View a timeline of your snapshots to see exactly when backups were created and by whom.
- **Zero Downtime:** Backups happen in the background without affecting your customer-facing speed.

---

## 🔄 2. Rapid Disaster Recovery (Restore)
In the unlikely event of a critical error, Tripbone allows you to "roll back time" with surgical precision.
- **Full Restoration:** Restore your entire ecosystem to a previous version in seconds.
- **Selective Recovery:** (Enterprise Only) Recover specific modules (e.g., just the Blog or just the Settings) without rolling back your recent bookings.
- **Sandbox Testing:** Restore your data to a staging environment to test major changes before they go live on your main domain.

---

## 📂 3. Universal Data Portability
You are never locked into a proprietary silo. Your data is yours to keep.
- **Export to JSON/CSV:** Download your entire database at any time for your own offline records or for use in external accounting software.
- **Media Asset Backup:** One-click download of all your uploaded tour images, blog thumbnails, and branding assets in a structured ZIP file.
- **Human-Readable Logs:** Audit logs that show every backup and restore action for total accountability.

---

## 🔒 4. Enterprise-Grade Redundancy
Leveraging the power of Google Cloud, your data is treated with the highest security standards.
- **Geo-Redundancy:** Backups are stored across multiple secure Google data centers, ensuring that even a regional infrastructure failure won't touch your business.
- **Encrypted at Rest:** Every snapshot is fully encrypted, ensuring that your guest PII remains private and secure.
- **Automated Intervals:** (Pro/Enterprise) Set your system to auto-backup every 24 hours, giving you a set-it-and-forget-it insurance policy.
