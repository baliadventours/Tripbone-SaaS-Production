## 🏔️ Case Study 1: Bali Adventours
**"From 5 Tours to an Empire"**
- **The Challenge:** High admin overhead for small group sizes and manual WhatsApp dispatching.
- **The Tripbone Solution:** Implemented the AItinerary Planner and Automated Dispatch.
- **The Result:** 
    - 40% reduction in manual admin time.
    - 35% increase in conversion rate via the AI Chatbot.
    - Successfully managing 50+ tours with the same staffing levels.
