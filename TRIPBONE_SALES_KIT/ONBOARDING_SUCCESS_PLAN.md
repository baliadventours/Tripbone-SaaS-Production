# Tripbone – Onboarding & Success Roadmap
**"From Purchase to First Booking in 48 Hours"**

Reducing the "Time-to-Value" is key for conversion. This document outlines the seamless onboarding process for new Tripbone owners.

---

## 🚀 Phase 1: The Foundation (Day 1)
- **Instant Deployment:** Your core engine is deployed to your domain. 
- **Brand Injection:** Upload your logo, set your colors, and connect your WhatsApp number.
- **Domain Mapping:** SSL certificates are generated automatically for your custom `tours.yourbrand.com` address.

---

## 🏗️ Phase 2: Inventory & Content (Day 1-2)
- **The "Great Migration":** Use our bulk import tools or the AI Tour Architect to populate your catalog.
- **AI Content Generation:** Generate your first 10 destination guides and 5 blog posts using the Gemini engine to establish immediate SEO authority.
- **Pricing Configuration:** Set up your tiered pricing (Adult/Child) and add-on services.

---

## 🔗 Phase 3: Integration & Launch (Day 2)
- **Payment Gateway Link:** Connect your Stripe or local gateway to start accepting real money.
- **WhatsApp Dispatch Test:** Train your guides on how to receive a "One-Click Dispatch" on their phones.
- **Go-Live:** Activate the **AItinerary** Agent and start driving traffic.

---

## 💎 The "Success Tier" Support
- **Full Video Knowledge Base:** Access to 50+ bite-sized tutorials covering every button in the dashboard.
- **Dedicated Discord/Slack:** Join a community of other Tripbone operators to share strategies and best practices.
- **Priority White-Glove (Enterprise Only):** We handle the migration, SEO setup, and staff training for you.
