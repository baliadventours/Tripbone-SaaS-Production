# Tripbone Landing Page Screenshot & Visual Asset Guide
**Target Domain: Tripbone.com**  
*This guide outlines the precise screens, focal points, and mockups to display on the promotions website to highlight our actual AI, WhatsApp, and Booking features.*

---

## 🎨 SCREENSHOT STRATEGY
*   **Aesthetic Principle:** Show real, elegant user interfaces. Use crisp, high-contrast, rounded cards framed in a soft shadow. Do not wrap files in generic computer frames.
*   **Callout Badges:** Place small rounded pointers (styled in bright Emerald Green `#059669` or energetic Coral) with thin lines to direct attention to core interactive elements.

---

## 📸 SCREENSHOT 1: THE AI TOUR GENERATOR (Feature Highlight 1)
*Focus: Creating a complete multi-day or day itinerary instantly from simple prompt configurations.*

*   **Mockup Concept:** A split side-by-side card.
    *   **Left Pane:** An elegant prompt builder with input bubbles:
        *   *Destination:* "Bali, Indonesia"
        *   *Duration:* "3 Days"
        *   *Theme:* "🌋 Adventure & Local Culture"
        *   *Special Requests:* "Honeymoon couple, private guide"
        *   *Button:* `[ Generate Itinerary ✨ ]` in loaded active state.
    *   **Right Pane:** A beautiful, structured itinerary card appearing seamlessly:
        *   *Header:* "Romantic Batur Climb & Cultural Snorkeling Experience"
        *   *Day 1 Timeline:* "04:00 AM Sunrise Jeep Trekking • 08:00 AM Coffee Plantation Breakfast • 01:00 PM Hidden Waterfall Swim."
*   **Target Callout Tag on Screen:**  
    `[ AI-GENERATED ITINERARIES ]` -> *"Generates clean, readable hourly agendas, photo highlights, and logistics outlines in standard Markdown."*

## 📸 SCREENSHOT 2: THE SMART BOOKING SYSTEM (Feature Highlight 2)
*Focus: Turning any travel website into a real-time reservation desk.*

*   **Mockup Concept:** The administrative dashboard calendar screen.
    *   Show a beautiful, structured calendar month grid decorated with colorful capsule indicators for scheduled trips (e.g., *"12 Active bookings"* under May 26th).
    *   A right-side sliding detail drawer showing an active booking slip: Lead Name, regular vs discount price indicators, checkout transaction source, and booking confirmation status.
*   **Target Callout Tag on Screen:**  
    `[ SIMPLIFIED ACCOUNTING & INVENTORY ]` -> *"No complicated setups. Track seat vacancies, dates, tour availabilities, and payments in one interface."*
